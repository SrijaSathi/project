Civil Engineering

VISION

To be a Department that strives towards quality education, research and consultancy in Civil Engineering.

MISSION

M1:To provide broad and high quality education to its students for a successful professional career.
M2: To serve the construction industry through dissemination of knowledge and technical services to rural community and professionals.
M3: To inculcate ethics and human values, effective communication and leadership qualities among students to meet the challenges of the society.

PEOs

Graduates of this program will be able to:
1.Analyze, formulate and solve Civil Engineering problems.
2.Excel in Civil Engineering and in other allied fields.
3.Demonstrate leadership qualities.
